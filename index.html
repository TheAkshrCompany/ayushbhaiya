About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Control and Dynamical Systems at California Institute of Technology, working on modeling and analysis of synthetic biological circuits. I am working with Richard Murray in the Biocircuits Lab. For more information on my research, please refer to the Publications page. I occasionally write informal technical posts on the Blog page.

I am working as an Adjunct Professor for Spring 2021-2022 semester at the Harvey Mudd College. I am teaching a course that I have designed on Introduction to Biological System Design.

In 2019, I graduated with a masters in Electrical Engineering at Caltech. Before that, in 2017, I graduated with a bachelors and a masters degree from the Indian Institute of Technoloy (IIT) Kharagpur, India. My Bachelor of Technology degree is in instrumentation engineering and Master of Technology in control systems engineering from the Electrical Engineering department.

Research Interests

  • Control theory
  • Systems and synthetic biology
  • Robustness of nonlinear systems
  • Artificial intelligence methods for systems modeling
